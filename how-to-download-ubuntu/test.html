    • Red Hat Enterprise Workstation 6.6, 6.7, 6.8, and 6.9 (64-bit)
    • SUSE Linux Enterprise 11.4 and 12.3 (64-bit)
    • CentOS 7.2, 7.3, 7.4, and 7.5 (64-bit)
    • CentOS 6.7, 6.8, and 6.9 (64-bit)
    • Ubuntu Linux 16.04.4, 18.04 LTS (64-bit)
